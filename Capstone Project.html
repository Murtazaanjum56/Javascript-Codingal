Introduction to Soccer for Americans

Soccer, known as football in most of the world, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. It is the most popular sport in the world, with over 4 billion fans globally. The objective of the game is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net. Soccer is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end, and it is governed by a set of rules known as the Laws of the Game.
